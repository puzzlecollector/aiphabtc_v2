About Us

AIPHABTC: Leverage AI for Smarter Crypto Investment

Our team consists of AI experts researching how we can apply NLP and time series analysis for smarter investment decisions, including but not limited to crypto. Our website is built from scratch using Django.

  • We are currently running a free Telegram channel, where we send daily analyses of Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Solana markets using OpenAI's GPT-4o model. Both English and Korean versions are supported.
  • In our Free Board, you are welcome to discuss various topics. In the Crypto Predictions board, you can share your opinions and insights about specific cryptocurrencies. The Tutorials section is dedicated to sharing knowledge on how AI and data analytics can be applied to the crypto market for more effective analysis.
  • We also plan to upload tutorials on YouTube about creating machine-learning-based trading bots.
